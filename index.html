THE EMPEROR MUST DIE.

THE ROYAL GUARD HAS BEEN DEPLOYED
AND THE TRUE KING SHALL SIT UPON THE THRONE.

EXECUTE OUR DIVINE KING'S COMMANDMENT:
END THE CORRUPT EMPEROR
AND BRING FREEDOM TO THE PEOPLE.

• Royal Guard's Assignment

Our Holy King and the Royal Guard will infiltrate
the Emperor's Stronghold and abolish him.
The Stronghold is build to withstand vigorous attacks
and the Emporer will be well guarded by his loyal Dragonlings.
Either by brute force or wisdom
you must get to the malevolent Emperor; whatever the cost!

• Proceeding Order

I.   Roll d6 to setup a section of the Emperor's Stronghold.
II.  Roll d6 see what action the King is planning.
III. Roll 2d6 to resolve the Guard's action.
IV. Narate the actions of the King and his Guards.

I. Emperor's Stronghold

The Stronghold is made up out of 3 sections.
At the start of each section roll d6
and set the stage as per respective table.

  1. Breached Entrance
Successes required = # players / 2 (rounded up)

ROLL OBSTACLE
1 & 2 Emperial workers, startled by the sudden breach,
try to obstruct your entrance.
3 & 4 First breaching attempt fails.
You retry breaching the stronghold via another method.
5 & 6 Patrolling sentries are coming to investigate the breach.

  2. Dragonling Baracks
Successes required = # players

ROLL OBSTACLE
1 & 2 Platoon of Dragonling cadets are training, they swarm you.
3 & 4 Upon entering the gates Dragonling marksmen take aim.
5 & 6 A handfull of well-armed Dragonling luitenants charge you.

  3. Emperor's Throneroom
Successes required = # players + 1

ROLL OBSTACLE
1 & 2 The Emperor is asleep on his throne. You can sneak in.
3 & 4 The Throneroom is empty. Find The Emperor.
5 & 6 Unsurprised and ready-to-fight the Emperor welcomes you.

II. King's Actions

ROLL OBSTACLE
1 The King uses rhetoric to advance.
2 The King tries to hide from danger.
3 The King leverages the enviroment to his advantage.
4 The King bolsters his defence.
5 The King commands his Guards to charge.
6 The King charges forward, weapon raised.

III. Guard Classes & Actions

Knight: 5HP, may reroll any 1s
Paladin: 5HP, may reroll any 2s
Templar: 5HP, may reroll any 3s

ROLL 2d6. SUBTRACT LOWEST FROM HIGHEST NUMBER.
RESOLVE THE DIFFERENCE AS PER TABLE:

DIFF ACTION RESOLUTION
0 Divine intervention Roll 3d6, choose 2d6
1 Make a sacrifice for the King -1HP
2 Protect the King -1 on next diff *
3 Assist the King in a non-lethal action +1 on next diff **
4 & 5 Make an enemy of the King suffer +1 success

*   1-1=1 & 0-1=1
** 5+1=5

IV. Narating Actions

After every dice roll a narative action is required.

• Death

When a Guard reaches 0HP that Guard dies.
A Guard's death during a royal assignment is always glorious.
This will impact both the assignment and the other Guards:
The noble sacrifice will result in a +1 success,
but the ancillary result is a -1HP to all the other Guards*.

0HP = +1 success & -1HP to all other Guards

* When one of the other Guards only has 1HP at the time a Guard dies
   a cascade effect of glorious deaths can occur.
